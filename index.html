Neeraj Chopra

India's Star Javelin Thrower

"Success is not an accident, it's hard work, perseverance, learning, studying, sacrifice, and most of all, love of what you are doing."

- Neeraj Chopra

Neeraj Chopra, born on December 24, 1997, is a prominent Indian athlete known for his exceptional prowess in javelin throwing. His dedication, hard work, and remarkable achievements have made him a source of inspiration for aspiring athletes around the world.
